## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This Design History File (DHF) has been compiled to satisfy the requirements of 21 CFR Part 820.30 (Design Controls) for the CardioGuard™ Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator (ICD), Model CG-7000 Series, in support of a Pre-Market Approval (PMA) application submitted to the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) under 21 CFR Part 814.

The DHF constitutes the complete, auditable record demonstrating that the design of the CG-7000 ICD was developed in accordance with approved design plans, that design outputs meet design inputs, that the device has been adequately verified and validated, and that appropriate risk management activities were conducted throughout the product development lifecycle.

This DHF has been organized to facilitate efficient FDA review during the PMA submission process and to support any subsequent inspections conducted under 21 CFR Part 820. Each DHF section maps directly to a specific design control requirement and, where applicable, to corresponding PMA Technical Section submissions per 21 CFR 814.20.

The CardioGuard™ CG-7000 is a Class III medical device subject to PMA under Section 515 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act). The device is classified under 21 CFR 870.3610 (Implantable pacemaker pulse generator) and 21 CFR 870.3800 (Cardioverter/defibrillator, implantable). A predicate device pathway under 510(k) is not applicable for this device class.

## 5. DOCUMENT ID NUMBERING SCHEME — OVERVIEW

Full details: see `docs/DHF/DHF-ICD-DOC-NUMBERING-SCHEME.md`

Pattern: `DHF-ICD-[SECT]-[SUBSECT]-[DOCTYPE]-[SEQ]-[REV]`

- **DHF-ICD** — Fixed prefix identifying this as a Design History File for the ICD program
- **[SECT]** — 3-digit section number (001–010)
- **[SUBSECT]** — 3-digit subsection number within the section (001–999)
- **[DOCTYPE]** — 3-letter document type code (PLN, REQ, RPT, PRT, REC, DWG, SPC, TST, LOG, MEM, CER, SOP, LBL, IDX)
- **[SEQ]** — 3-digit sequential number for same-type documents within a subsection (001–999)
- **[REV]** — Single uppercase letter revision designator (A=Initial, B=First revision, etc.)
