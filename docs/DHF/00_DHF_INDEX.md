## DHF Purpose and Scope

This Design History File (DHF) has been compiled in accordance with 21 CFR 820.30 (Design Controls) and 21 CFR 820.3(e) (Definition of DHF) and documents the design history of the CardioSync CRT-D Model CS-9000. The DHF demonstrates that the device was designed in accordance with the approved design plan and that design controls were properly implemented throughout the design and development lifecycle.

This DHF supports the Premarket Approval (PMA) Application P240001 submitted to the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) under 21 CFR Part 814. All documents referenced herein are maintained in the NovaCor Document Control System (NDCS) and are subject to change control per SOP-QMS-0025 (Document and Record Control).

## DHF Section Descriptions

### DHF-00: DHF Index and Compilation (820.30(j))
The master index and compilation of the complete Design History File. Includes this index document, the document numbering scheme, the section register, the PMA cross-reference map, and the requirements traceability matrix index.

### DHF-01: Design Planning (820.30(a))
Documents establishing the design and development plan, including project phases, resource allocation, team responsibilities, milestone schedule, risk management integration, and design review schedule.

### DHF-02: Design Input (820.30(c))
Documents defining the design input requirements, including intended use, user needs, performance requirements, safety requirements, reliability requirements, regulatory requirements, and all requirement sources with stakeholder input records.

### DHF-03: Design Output (820.30(d))
Documents constituting the design output, including device description, engineering drawings, bill of materials, software architecture documents, manufacturing specifications, and the device master record index.

### DHF-04: Design Review (820.30(e))
Records of all formal design reviews conducted during development, including phase gate reviews, interim reviews, and final design review. Each review record includes participants, agenda, meeting minutes, action items, and sign-off signatures.

### DHF-05: Design Verification (820.30(f))
Documents confirming that design outputs meet design inputs, including the verification plan, individual test protocols and reports for all verification activities (electrical, mechanical, software, EMC, electrical safety, reliability).

### DHF-06: Design Validation (820.30(g))
Documents confirming that the device consistently produces a device conforming to defined user needs and intended uses, including simulated use testing, animal studies, clinical study reference (IDE G240001), usability/human factors validation, and software validation.

### DHF-07: Design Transfer (820.30(h))
Documents ensuring that the device design is correctly translated into production specifications, including manufacturing process validation, transfer review records, and production readiness assessment.

### DHF-08: Design Changes (820.30(i))
Records of all design changes implemented after initial design freeze, including change request forms, impact assessments, re-verification/re-validation summaries, and approval records.
